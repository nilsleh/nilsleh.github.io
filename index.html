Nils Lehmann

I am currently a PhD Student at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), supervised by Prof. Jonathan Bamber and Prof. Xiaoxiang Zhu, working on uncertainty quantification for sea-level rise. More broadly my interests lie in Bayesian Deep Learning, uncertainty quantification and generative modeling for Earth Observational data. I am also very passionate about open-source software and a maintainer of TorchGeo and author of Lighting-UQ-Box.
